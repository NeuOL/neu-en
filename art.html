Northeastern University

College of Arts

Northeastern University College of Arts was founded in June 2001. The college is constructed to meet the planning and development strategies of NEU, adapt to the social and educational development needs, comply the structure regulation of discipline, strengthen the reasonable distribution of subject and improve the educational environment. NEU College of Arts originally has carried on the Motto Spirit -"Make self-reinforcing forever, take knowledge and practice to be together" of NEU, fulfilled the construction principle- "Based on talent, based on character, based on culture and based on management" of the college, and practiced the Motto conception-" Take the unity of arts and science, uphold the virtue to make the behavior nice, exert the arts to perfection, bring the harmony and common prosperity" of college. Moreover, the college has been taken the responsibility on bringing up the compound artistic talents that have the expertise, the high overall quality and the innovated capability, popularizing the arts knowledge and improving the cultural quality of NEU. For ten years of development of the college, the overall operation has achieved rapid development.

It is significant to found an arts college in multi-disciplinary university. The studies of scientific knowledge and artistic promote each other heavily. It is helpful for the nurture of scientific talents and artistic talents. With the grumous scientific atmosphere, the multi-disciplinary superiority, as well as the great efforts made by the leaders, faculty and students, NEU College of Arts persists that combining the science and art, popularization and improvement, moral and talent; and forms the teaching characteristics that take combination of science and art, professional qualities and cultural quality. The college has achieved a series of fruitful results, e.g. teaching, talent nurture, scientific research, academic exchanges, and disciplinary construction.

There are two primary academic organizations for the College of Arts, i.e.the Design Department and the Music Department. The Music Department offers music professionals (including 4 professional, such as direction piano, vocal, Chinese musical instruments and western musical instruments), and the Design Department offers two undergraduate majors that are environmental design and visual communication design. In 2006, the college was grantedtwo rights of second-class master degree-musicology and art design. In 2011, the college was granted the rights of first-class master degree which is Design, and retained grant rights of second-class master degree-musicology. More than 540 undergraduates and 50 graduates are studying in the college. The college also annually selects outstanding students to learn and exchange abroad, and recommends about 15% of graduates to pursuing a master degree in NEU or the other universities.

During the reform and practice in teaching, the college lays stress on establishing the noble education concept, which is to foster the innovative artistic talents, to construct the training program that both pays attention on the professional quality with knowledge, ability, diathesis and the cultural quality. The college forms an energetic and younger teachers' team that has strong professional basis and research potential. Currently, the college has 57 full-time teachers, including 9 professors, 11 associate professors. The teachers who have a doctorate or master account for 68% of the total number, and who have abroad masters and doctorate account for over 30% of total number. Many well-known scholars have been teaching in the college, and established several national and provincial quality courses, video open courses and resource sharing courses. A Number of teachers and students win the prize in the domestic and abroad competitions.

The college built a series of good teaching facilities, such as the special classrooms, piano rooms, concert halls, computer classrooms, digital design laboratory, photography studio and library. Nowadays, the college has Steinway pianos, 3D engraving machine, 3D printers, high-end color printing copiers, recording systems, audio workstations and other teaching equipment. The total worth of the teaching instruments is more than five million RMB, and the art audiovisual material cost more than 300 thousand RMB. All of these bring a good foundation conditions for art teaching, research and disciplines construction.

The college strengthens the foreign exchange to create a strong academic atmosphere. The college has invited some national celebrities and the experts from USA, France, Germany, Russia, Hong Kong, Taiwan and other countries and regions to give lectures. In 2007, the college reached a cooperation exchange agreement with the United States Illinois Northeastern University, and annually selects outstanding students to exchange and study in the USA.

The college pays attention to take the peculiar advantages of specialty, and make the contribution to the construction of university culture. The college emphases on the cultural construction of "harmonious beauty", strives to create the artistic atmosphere with visual beauty and auditory beauty, and advocate the teachers and students to discover beauty in the art ocean, to appreciate beauty in the arts and cultural exchanges, to pursue beauty in the art peak climbing and to create beauty in the practice of artistic inspiration. During these processes, the construction of college culture is achieved harmoniously.

In the future, the college will focus on the key breakthrough, the characteristics strengthening, coordinated development and overall improvement. The college will further improve the training system of artistic talents, explore the training mode of artistic talents that has innovative spirit and capability. It will also further select features, design features, create features, maintain features and enhance features, so that the college can take the construction of discipline and the promotion of scientific research capability as breakthrough, drive the overall level and strength of teaching to be raised, effectively improve the teaching quality, and enhance the overall competitiveness of disciplines.
