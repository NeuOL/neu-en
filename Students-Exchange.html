Long-term Students Exchange Program

NEU has carried out the student exchange with 27 overseas partner institutions, in order to broaden the horizon of students and provide a multi-culture education environment. Every semester excellent students will be selected and sent abroad for exchange study. In 2012, NEU has sent 122 exchange students for a long-term exchange for more than one semester. Through the exchange study the students has gained the knowledge, friendship, and the experience of overseas culture and the international communication.
