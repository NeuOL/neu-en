Brief Introduction of H. L. Chang Education Foundation

As a great patriot and hero of China, H. L. Chang loved his nation’s education industry throughout his life. Since 1820s he had donated most of his father’s inheritance to help poor children to enter school, and found “Xinmin Primary School” and “Tongze Middle School” in many areas of northeast China. He was also the president of Northeastern University from 1928 to 1936, and then became honored president of the board of the university.

In honor of Mr. H. L. Chang’s historical contribution to the education industry and even the whole nation, to develop his spirit of “Love our school, hometown, country and then human”, mobilize all circles in the country and oversea, to support the education industry, especially the develop of his beloved Northeastern University and to train and bring up elite for the invigoration of China, Northeastern University found the H. L. Chang Education Foundation by cooperate with Mr. Chang’s relatives and friends, as well as all circles of the society.

H. L. Chang Education Foundation, founded in Shenyang in 2006 is a non-public welfare nationwide foundation, issued by China Ministry of Education and registered by Ministry of Civil Affairs. The board of the foundation is composed by Mr. Chang’s family members, all circles in the country and oversea and leaders of Northeastern University. The honored chairpersons are Mr. Robert L Chang, Mrs. Luying Chang—Mr. H. L. Chang’s son and daughter, Mr. Yan Mingfu—former vice chairman of CPPCC, and Director of United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, former Director China Charity Federation , and Mr. Choi Koon Shum, CEO of Sun Wah Group in Hong Kong. President of Northeastern University will take the position of chairperson. The office of the foundation located in NEU will be in charge of the general affairs. The foundation will be organized under a member system. The name, honor and the rights of the members will be issued according to the total sum of the donation of institutions and single persons.

The funds’ resources of H. L. Chang Education Foundation are the donation of domestic and oversea organizations and single persons. The usage of the funds will be completed through the foundation’s certain services, according to the charter and mainly respecting the intension of the donators. The range of the usage including: importing the special elites, rewarding excellent students, carrying out academic exchanges, improving the teaching condition, subsidizing poor students and other projects conforming to the charter.

The foundation of H. L. Chang Education Foundation got the attention and help of people who are enthusiastic to public welfare and the China education industry, and the alumni of NEU. We welcome warmly more attention and support to H. L. Chang Education Foundation.
