Northeastern University

Disciplinary Introduction

Northeastern University (abbreviated as NEU) has a variety of disciplines and specialties, covering 9 major categories, i.e. philosophy, economics, law, education, literature, natural science, engineering, management science, and art. The school boasts 21 first-level discipline doctoral degree programs, 100 second-level discipline doctoral degree programs, 37 first-level discipline mater degree programs, 181 second-level discipline master degree programs, and 66 bachelor degree programs. It has 15 post-doctoral research stations, 3 national key disciplines (covering 11 second-level disciplines), 4 second-level national key disciplines, 1 national key cultivating discipline, 8 first-level Liaoning provincial key disciplines, and 18 second-level Liaoning provincial key disciplines, and 1 Liaoning provincial key cultivating discipline.
