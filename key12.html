Key Laboratory

Liaoning Provincial Key Laboratory of Integrated Information Processing System for Digitalized Equipments

Based on the disciplines of Computer Science and Technology, Information and Communication Engineering of Northeastern University, the laboratory studies the integrated information processing technology for digital equipments. The laboratory is aimed at promoting industrialization by informationalization and rejuvenating the old industrial base in the Northeast according to the characteristics of equipment manufacturing industry in Liaoning province. Researches of the laboratory include distributed cooperative computing technology, new data management and intelligent information processing technology, new network and information security technology, embedded system and real-time system technology.

The laboratory has more than ten high-level talents, including scholars in Thousand Talents Program funded by Central Organization Department, members of the Academic Degree Committee of the State Council, winners of China National Funds for Distinguished Young Scientists and National Outstanding Youth Funds and scholars in New Century Excellent Talents Program. The laboratory insists to build advanced research groups by taking outstanding academic leaders as the core and fostering young talents as the emphasis. It currently has two science and technology innovation teams of Liaoning Provincial Department of Education.

Since established, the laboratory has obtained over 100 important projects, including the project of National 973 Program, the project of National 863 Program, the project of China National Funds for Distinguished Young Scientists, the key projects of National Natural Science Foundation of China, et al. The laboratory has gained lots of rewards for great academic achievements, such as the second prize of National Scientific and Technological Progress Award, the second prize of the Natural Science Progress Award of the Ministry of Education, the first prize of Scientific and Technological Progress Award of Liaoning Province. Besides, over 50 top-level academic papers were published in SIGMOD、VLDB、ICDE、RTSS、ACL、TKDE, et al. Two graduates received the Outstanding Doctoral Dissertation Nomination Award of China, and three graduates received the Outstanding Doctoral Dissertation Award of China Computer Federation.
