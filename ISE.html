College of Information Science and Engineering

College of Information Science and Engineering of Northeastern University (referred to as the college hereafter) was founded in October 1996 by combining two strong departments, Department of Automation and Department of Computer Science and Engineering. Its origin dates back to the Department of Electrical Engineering set up in the early 20th century when Northeastern University was initially established.

The college currently has 18 research institutes, 1 national electronic experiment teaching demonstration center, 1 national electric and electronic education base, 1 experiment center and the editorial office of the journal of Control and Decision.

The college has 422 faculty and staff, including 2 academicians from Chinese Academy of Sciences and Chinese Academy of Engineering, 2 members of Discipline Assessment Group of State Council, 5 on the list of " Thousand Talents Scheme" of Chinese Organization Department of the CPC Central Committee, 11 Cheung Kong Scholars, 1 distinguished professor, 8 recipients of State Excellent Youth Fund (including 2 for Overseas Excellent Youth Fund), 28 talents listed into "New Century Talents" of Chinese Ministry of Education, 64 Ph.D. supervisors, 74 professors and 117 associate professors. The college provides 8 undergraduate programs, including automation, computer science and technology, communications engineering, electronic information engineering, monitoring and control technology and equipment, electronic engineering and automation, electronic science and technology and cyber physical system engineering.

Standing on the platform of "211 Project" and "985 Project", the college continually optimizes its discipline structure and strengthens the construction of discipline connotation, displaying its edge in science of information. The college now has 6 first-level disciplines, 15 second-level disciplines, 3 first-level doctoral degree programs, 2 mobile post-doctoral stations and 11 second-level doctoral degree programs. Control science and engineering is first-level national key discipline; computer applied technology is second-level national key discipline; control science and engineering and computer science and technology are key disciplines of Liaoning Province. The college has 1 national key laboratory, covering national key laboratory of "New Technology for Software", 3 national engineering research centers, 2 engineering research centers of the Ministry of Education, 5 key laboratories of Liaoning Province and 1 engineering and technology Center of Liaoning Province. The college undertakes 2 major discipline projects supported by "211 Project". One of them, third-stage construction project, "Basic Theory and Key Technology Research in Integrated Automation System of Complex Industrial Process" ranks the 4th in terms of "construction effect" of all the 98 projects in the field of information when they're being checked before delivery. The other project, "Basic Theory and Key Technology in New Generation Network Information Processing" ranks the 53rd. The college also undertakes 2 science and technology platforms construction supported by "Project 985 (2012-2020)". The college has 2 innovation teams funded by the National Natural Science Foundation of China, 1 innovation team funded by the Ministry of Education

With its permanent emphasis on teaching and research, the college has yielded many results which take the lead both nationally and internationally and outstanding experts. The college has undertaken 1881 funding and research projects since 2000, among which 945 are vertical projects. The college has won 3 second prize for State Technological Invention Awards, 6 second prize for national technology advancement, 68 prizes for provincial technology advancement and obtained 235 authorized patents. The cumulative funds are up to RMB 360 million. The number of the published research papers is 10571. Among those papers, 5760 papers are indexed by SCI, EI and ISTP. 171 monographs and textbooks have been published.

In the past years, the college has obtained fruitful results in teaching and research, including 1 first-level and 3 second-level national teaching achievement prize, 5 first-level and 7 second-level provincial teaching achievement prizes, 8 national elite courses and 8 provincial elite courses, 1 national elite course books and 5 provincial elite course books,16 course books listed among national 11th and 12th "Five-year-plan" planned course books, 7 provincial awards for distinguished teachers, 4 members of Teaching Guidance Committee of Chinese Ministry of Education. The discipline of automation is approved to be the national innovation zone for talents cultivation; the disciplines of automation, computer science and technology and electronic information engineering are approved to be the distinctive disciplines by the Ministry of Education. It has 1 national and 2 provincial teaching teams; it undertakes 1 national program for teaching reform, 137 projects of "National College Students Innovation Experiment Programs" and 10 Liaoning provincial programs for teaching reform.

The college currently has 5575 students with 3083 undergraduates and 2392 postgraduates. Since 2000, 1 student Youth League branch has won "May 4th Red Flag Youth League" of China; 4 students have won "Chinese Youth Science and Technology Innovation Prize"; 1 student has won "National Excellent PhD Dissertation Award"; 4 students have won "National Excellent PhD Dissertation Nomination Award"; 6 students have been awarded "PhD Students New Academic Talent Prize" by the Ministry of Education; 5 students have been awarded the Special Prize of "Excellent Student Scholarships" by BaoGang Education Fund; 1168 students have won national or international prizes in competitions, including "Challenge Cup" National Electronic Designing Competition and American Math Modeling Competition. The average first-time employment rate for undergraduates exceeds 95% for several consecutive years. The students who are recommended to or recruited through exams by the graduate school account for 40% of the total of students and those recommended to other universities are all accepted by the first-class universities in China including Tsinghua University and Beijing University, etc. 5% of undergraduate students go abroad for further study annually.

The college has been developing cooperative research and academic exchanges with colleges and universities and research institutions from more than twenty countries including the United States, the United Kingdom, Germany, France, Japan, Australia and Russia. Many world-renowned experts and scholars have been employed by our college as honorary professors and part-time professors. Each year, a group of overseas scholars will be invited to give lectures in the college and some of our teachers will be sent abroad to give lectures and develop cooperation and communications.

The college has always born in mind the mission of "promoting the development of information science, spreading advanced university culture and cultivating excellent information talents", persisting in its concept of "putting people and education first and pursing harmonious development between collective and individual" and developing its spirit of "being strong in action, daring to be pioneers, striving for harmony but not sameness and being prepared for possible setbacks". With discipline construction as the lead, with team construction as the core, with talent cultivation as the foundation, with scientific management and culture construction as the guarantee, with quality construction as the breakthrough, the college has been upgrading its discipline connotation and has made outstanding achievements in discipline construction, teaching and research, talent cultivation and school-running level. The college has been awarded the honorary titles of "Advanced CPC Party Committee" and "Advanced Collective" 22 times since 2001 by College Working Committee of Liaoning Provincial Party Committee, the Education Department of Liaoning Province, Shenyang municipal government, Shenyang Science and Education Working Committee and Northeastern University respectively. The college workers' union has been elected as the Advanced Branch Workers' Union of NEU 6 times. It was granted the title of Model Workers' Family by Workers' Union at national, provincial and municipal levels in 2005, 2007 and 2008 respectively. The college Youth League has been awarded 5 times the title of May 4th Red Flag Youth League by Shenyang Municipal Youth League Committee, 6 times the title of Advanced Youth League by Northeastern University. Facing new opportunities and challenges, the college will move forward with full passion. Centering on the orientation of NEU as "domestically first-class and internationally renowned high level research university, which takes the lead in China's new industrialization", the college will unite and lead its faculty and students, striving for the goal of "building a domestically first-class and internationally renowned college of information."
