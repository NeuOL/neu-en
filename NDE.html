Northeastern University

The College of National Defense Education

The program of national defense students began in 1999 at the Northeastern University which, as one of the first universities, military command entrusts to cultivate national defense students in the whole nation. And it is the first university with which the Shenyang Military Command signed the contract to cultivate national defense students. It has produced many outstanding individuals in the Shenyang Military Command and even in the national one, such as Li Xiaodong, Ding Ning, Jiang Yantao, Zhang Lianxu and so on. The Machinery Class 0401 of College of National Defense Education was honored The Advanced Class of Liaoning Province. In order to enhance the unique cultivation of national defense students and meet the strategic needs of qualified talents, the Northeastern University integrates the resources related with the national defense education into the foundation of the College of National Defense Education in 2010.

There are 323 national defense students, 312 boys and 11 girls, respectively. There are 9 programs including Communication Engineering, Software Engineering, Computer Science and Technique, Electric Work and Automation, Mechanical Engineering and Automation, Material Science and Engineering, Surveying and Mapping Engineering, Civil Engineering, Accounting. There are 15 classes in all.

The College of National Defense Education won the reward in honor of "Enrich the Nation and Strengthen the Military Command, Construct the Great Wall Together" in Liaoning Province. And Computer Class 0801 and Mechanical Engineering and Automation Class 1001 won the honor of Advanced Class of National Defense Students in Shenyang Military Command. And Communication Engineering Class 0901 was awarded The Advanced Classes at the Northeastern University. Zhang Dongdong and other 5 students won the honor of Topnotch National Defense Students of Liaoning Province.

The College of National Defense Education unified management and organization for national defense students, which is a strong backing of deepening both the school and military. Also, it is a significant measure to strengthen military training and education in the protection. To get self-improving in literature and military and then to rule the world is the target of the College of National Defense Education, which is the call of the times with a long way to go.
