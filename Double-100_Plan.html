Double-100 Plan

"Double Hundred" Foreign Teachers Recruitment Plan

The project of "Double Hundred" is conceived according to the Program of NEU Reform and Development of 12th Five-Year Plan. That is setting up 100 courses instructed in English while recruiting 100 foreign teachers of non-Chinese origin. "Double Hundred" foreign teachers are teachers working in all the subjects of the university. The employment period is 1-3 years.

I.Employment Level and Criteria:

1.Foreign teachers will work as professors, associate professors and lecturers.

2.Criteria

①Basic requirements:
Abide by the relevant law and regulation of People's Republic of China;
With a high degree of professionalism, team work, collective and devoted spirit.
Honor the academic standards and specifications.
Have a doctoral degree.
Healthy enough to fulfill the requirements of the job.

②Age: Doctoral graduates and post doctoral researchers should be no older than 35; associate professors should be no older than 55; professors should be no older than 65.

③Other requirements: All the degrees of the candidates should be issued by elite overseas universities. Candidates should be able to teach in English.

II.Salary and Benefits

  • 1.Foreign teachers must work full-time for NEU. The salary is protocol wage.

  • 2.The annual salary for a professor is 300000-700000 RMB; the annual salary for an associate professor is 200000-400000 RMB; the annual salary for a lecturer is 100000-200000 RMB. The salary standard will be decided after consultation between two parties of the employment contract, with consideration for the candidates'longevity, academic level and teaching and research tasks.

  • 3.The salary of foreign teachers will be paid monthly following the annual salary system.

  • 4.Foreign teachers' income tax will be held by NEU according to the Individual Income Tax Law of the People"s Republic of China.

  • 5.NEU will provide foreign teachers with health and injury insurance according to relevant policies of national and local authorities.
