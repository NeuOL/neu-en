The Thousand-Talents Plan (The Recruitment Program of Global Experts)

Intro

The Recruitment Program of Global Experts which is also called the Thousand-Talents Plan is organized by the Central Coordination Committee of the Chinese Communist Party. The program will last for 5 to 10 years and has been carried out since 2008. It aims to recruit overseas top scientists and talents back to China.

It is hoped that the recruited talents will lead the innovative industries, improve key technologies and develop the high-tech industries in State Key Labs, National Key Innovative Programs, State Key Subjects, National Enterprises, State-Owned Commercial and Financial Institutes and High-tech Development Zones

It mainly includes Long Term Project and Short Term Project.

Requirement

I. Long Term Project Applicant

  • 1. Have gained Ph.D. from abroad.
  • 2. Does not exceed 55 years old. (generally no more than 60 years old in humanities and social science)
  • 3. Have served as professor or equivalent at world famous universities, research institutions, enterprise, or financial institutions.
  • 4. Possess world leading research ability in the fields of natural science, engineering, finance, management and etc.
  • 5. Have published academic papers of significant impact in recognized journals, or received international respected awards, or possess core technology in the field of science and engineering within the past 5 years.

II. Long Term Project Applicant

  • 1. Applicants for short-term projects must hold leading positions in the fields considered to be national priority, including science, technology, and industrial development. Satisfy the requirements as indicated in Recruit Administration of High Level Talents from Overseas. (Refer to details for I-1)
  • 2. Have fixed workplace contract in China, with determined objectives, whilst showing ability to contribute.
  • 3. Must have collaborated with the intended department for a period of 3 years minimum. The applicant must also spend no less than 2 month annually working in China, with clear indication regarding the ownership of intellectual property gained during that time.

Treatment

I. Long Term Project Finalists

  • 1.National finance provide 1 million yuan RMB for once.
  • 2.Northeastern University provide research funding of 10 million yuan RMB.
  • 3.Northeastern University provide annual pay of 800 thousand yuan RMB.
  • 4.Northeastern University provide apartment-renting subsidy of 3 thousand yuan RMB every month.

II. Short Term Project Finalists

  • 1.National finance provide 500 thousand yuan RMB for once.
  • 2.Northeastern University provide monthly pay of 70 thousand yuan RMB every working month at NEU.
  • 3.Northeastern University provide apartment-renting subsidy of 3 thousand yuan RMB every working month at NEU.
