The National Postdoctoral International Exchange Program Winners

Requirements & Qualifications

  1. The applicants should generally be under 35 years old.
  2. Have obtained Ph. D. degree from the world's top 100 universities.
  3. Achieved outstanding research achievements during Ph.D study.
  4. Non-native English speakers should have good Chinese (or English) skills in listening, speaking, reading and writing.
  5. Non-native English speakers should have good Chinese (or English) skills in listening, speaking, reading and writing.

Other Requirements

  1. The talents introduced should work under the management of postdoctoral researchers in stations.
  2. The period of exchange will be two years. The talents introduced shall ensure that his postdoctoral research in China is no fewer than 24 months.
  3. The talents introduced need to sign an agreement with the university. The content of the agreement contains the regulations of rights and duties of both parties, research contents, time limit, assessment, treatment, achievement, liability for breach of contract and so on.

Relevant Treatment

Everyone will be sponsored with 300, 000 yuan per year.
