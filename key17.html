Key Laboratory of Dynamics Reliability and Quality Engineering of Major Machinery and Equipment (Liaoning Province)

The Liaoning Provincial Key Laboratory “Dynamics reliability and quality engineering of major machinery and equipment” rely on the “mechanical design and theory” which is the national key discipline of school of mechanical engineering and automation Northeastern University. The laboratory has good research foundation on the fields of kinetics and dynamic design of major machinery and equipment, reliability design and life prediction of major machinery and equipment, quality engineering of major machinery and equipment and so on. The comprehensive laboratory covers the main research areas of mechanical engineering having unique advantages. It takes theoretical research as the source to highlight fundamental, regards technology research as the principal part to emphasize perspectiveness and perceives applied research as export to reflect strategic. It establishes the operating mechanism of "open, flow, unite and competition", creates favorable scientific research environment and experimental conditions, and promotes the improvement of the overall level of the laboratory. It is made as the top-ranking major machinery and equipment research and talent training base to representing the academic, experimental and management level of the province or even our country, and servicing the national strategy of development and the revitalization of northeast old industrial base. The laboratory construction aims at cultivating big team, undertaking big subjects, solving big problems, making great achievements, realizing great developments and eventually building a domestic first-class advanced comprehensive technical support system with the integrating functions of modern design, manufacturing, analysis and experiment.
