Overseas 100-Talents Plan

The Hundred-Overseas Young talents Introduction Plan of Northeastern University

The plan is abroad on the Young Project of the 1000-Talents Plan.

I．Goals of the Plan:

    Generally introduce one hundred overseas young talents , short for the Overseas 100-Talents Plan.

II．Requirement:

  • 1. In the well-known universities, research institutions or overseas well-known enterprise institution has a formal teaching or research position.
  • 2. PhD in overseas well-known colleges or universities, and have 3 years working experience in overseas scientific research; Or PhD degree in domestic famous universities, and have 5 years working experience in overseas research.
  • 3. Age is not more than 40 years old.

III．Selection process:

  • 1. The Northeastern University released recruitment jobs publicly.
  • 2. The young talents as the Young Project of the 1000-Talents Plan candidates submitted to the ministry of education to participate in the review.
  • 3. Through the Young Project of the 1000-Talents Plan reviewer, Northeastern University hired as the youth distinguished professor.
  • 4. About not through the Young Project of the 1000-Talents Plan review special young overseas high-level talents, Northeastern University organization participate expert on the academic review. If through the review， Northeastern University hired as the youth distinguished associate professor.

IV．Treatment

  • 1. The personnel's the Hundred-Overseas Young Talents Plan youth distinguished professor, associate professor of distinguished as PhD supervisor.
  • 2. The young distinguished professor will be given a salary of 250 thousands yuan RMB per year, the youth distinguished associate professor is 200 thousands yuan RMB per year.
  • 3. Northeastern University is included in the plan personnel with necessary living conditions and scientific research conditions.
  • 4. To provide young distinguished professor of not less than 600 thousands yuan RMB housing subsidies, and one-time for it.
       Scientific research supporting funds provide not less than 1 million yuan RMB.
  • 5. For young distinguished associate professor provide not less than 500 thousands RMB housing subsidies, and one-time provide not less than 800 thousands yuan RMB of supporting scientific research funds.
  • 6. Priority support for the plan of personnel to join both academicians, ChangJiang scholars distinguished professor, national outstanding youth science fund gainer high-level leadership team, and support them in interdisciplinary and emerging disciplines to form innovative academic team.
  • 7. Within the period of youth caries distinguished associate professor can attend Northeastern University professor.
