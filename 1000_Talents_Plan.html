National "Thousand Talents Plan" Innovation Long Term Project for Distinguished Experts

Disciplinary Fields

Natural Science, Engineering Technology, Economics and Finance, Management Science, etc.

Requirements

  1. Have gained Ph.D degree from abroad and under 55 years old.
  2. Have served as professor or equivalent at world famous universities, research institutions, enterprises, or financial institutions.
  3. Have reached the world first-class research level in his or her field and have published academic papers of significant impact in recognized journals, or received international respected awards, or possess core technology in the field of science and engineering in the latest 5 years.
  4. Work full-time for NEU, Once introduced.

Preferential Treatments

  1. Northeastern University provides necessary scientific research conditions, assigns research assistants and assists in building academic team.
  2. Northeastern University offers no less than 10 million research fund ( Natural science, Engineering Technology).
  3. Northeastern University offers annual pay of 800,000 yuan besides one-time subsidy of 1 million yuan provided by the central government.
  4. Northeastern University offers apartment-renting subsidy of 3,000 yuan RMB per month.

Application Date

Please contact us and the exact application time in national notice shall prevail. For more details please check: http://www.1000plan.org/qrjh/section/2.

National "Thousand Talents Plan" Innovation Short Term Project for Distinguished Experts

Disciplinary Fields

atural science, Engineering Technology, Economics and Finance, Management Science, etc.

Requirements

  1. Have gained Ph.D degree from abroad and under 55 years old.
  2. Have served as professor or equivalent at world famous universities, research institutions, enterprises, or financial institutions.
  3. Have reached the world first-class research level in his field and have published academic papers of significant impact in recognized journals, or received international respected awards, or possess core technology in the field of science and engineering in the latest 5 years.
  4. Applicants for short-term projects must hold leading positions in the fields considered to be national priority, including science, technology, and industrial development.
  5. Shall work at the intended department for no less than 2 months annually within the first three years, once introduced.

Preferential Treatments

  1. Northeastern University provides necessary scientific research conditions.
  2. Northeastern University provides monthly pay of 70,000 yuan RMB every working month at NEU besides one-time subsidy of 1 million yuan provided by China central government.
  3. Northeastern University provides apartment-renting subsidy of 3,000 yuan RMB every working month at NEU.

Application Date

Please contact us and the exact application time in national notice shall prevail. For more details please check: http://www.1000plan.org/qrjh/section/2.
